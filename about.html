About STF

Our Story

Built on Trust,
Driven by Detail.

Sweep the Floor Cleaning Co. was founded right here in Branson, Missouri with one goal: provide property owners, Airbnb hosts, contractors, and business owners with a cleaning service they can truly depend on.

We started with a single crew and an obsession with getting every detail right. Word spread. Clients stayed. Today, we are proud to be one of the most trusted cleaning services in the Ozarks — still locally owned, still personally invested in every job we take.

We do not use gimmicks or complicated pricing structures. We show up, we clean thoroughly, and we make sure every client walks into a space they are proud of.
